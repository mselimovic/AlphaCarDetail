Services

Some of services that we offer

Professional Polishing

Car polishing is the process of removing minor clear coat damage (scratches and swirl marks) using a polishing liquid to flatten the clear coat which increases the gloss-level. The polishing process can be performed by machine or by hand and should be followed by protecting the paint using a wax, sealant or coating.

Ceramic Coating

Industry-grade ceramic coating is a chemical polymer solution that is applied to the exterior of a vehicle to protect it from external paint damage. Typically applied by hand, it blends with the paint of your car and creates an additional hydrophobic layer of protection.

Interior Cleaning

Car interior detailing is all about beauty on the inside. The inside of a car should provide a hygienic environment with a clean, fresh smell to help create a positive driving experience. This involves deep-cleaning techniques that sanitize upholstery, carpets and surfaces for a germ-free finish. Interior detailing to a high standard requires preparation, hard work and a standardized process. If you want immaculate results, work through a series of steps to ensure that no corner of the car is overlooked.

Engine Bay Cleaning

Unlike a cars exterior, which we regularly wash, engine bays arent pampered nearly as much. Without attention, that dirt, debris, and oil can gunk up your cars workings, potentially start a fire, and cause engine, steering, and suspension issues. For those who have never washed a cars engine bay before, dont worry we will do it for you, and your ride will come out the other side looking fresh and clean.
